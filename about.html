Meet Our Founder

Kaleigh Smith

Owner & Nurse Practitioner

When I opened the doors to K | Luxe Wellness, my vision was deeply personal: to create an environment where intentional wellness and elevated care exist in seamless harmony. A space where you feel genuinely seen, thoughtfully understood, and exceptionally cared for at every step of your journey.

With over 11 years of healthcare experience spanning emergency medicine and medical aesthetics, I bring both clinical precision and compassionate insight to every patient relationship. My philosophy is rooted in the understanding that true beauty reflects balance, vitality, and confidence. Through personalized, medically guided treatments, I focus on enhancing your natural features while supporting your overall well-being from the inside out.

At K | Luxe Wellness, care is never transactional — it is intentional. Each treatment plan is thoughtfully designed, every detail carefully considered, and every experience elevated to reflect the highest standards of excellence. From your first consultation to your final follow-up, you can expect discretion, expertise, and unwavering attention to detail.

It is a privilege to be entrusted with your care. I look forward to welcoming you into a space created to support your health, refine your confidence, and empower you to step fully into your most vibrant self.

— Kaleigh

Our Journey

The K | Luxe Story

K | Luxe Wellness was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves access to elevated healthcare. What started as a dream in 2019 became a reality in 2026, when we opened our doors in Denver, Colorado.

From the beginning, our mission has been clear— to create a space where clinical expertise meets refined comfort. A place where advanced, results-driven treatments are delivered with warmth, intention, and uncompromising attention to detail. Here, you are never just an appointment; you are a valued partner in your own wellness journey.

Today, K | Luxe has evolved into a trusted destination for individuals seeking transformative outcomes within an environment that feels both sophisticated and welcoming. While we continue to grow, our foundation remains the same: intentionality, empowerment, and confidence guide every decision, every treatment, and every experience we create.
